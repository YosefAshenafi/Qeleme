PRIVACY POLICY FOR QELEM
Last updated: Dec 11, 2025

ABOUT QELEM
Qelem is an educational platform designed to provide interactive learning experiences for Ethiopian students of all ages through multiple choice questions, flashcards, quizzes, and homework assistance.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
Qelem requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:
- Homework Assistance: Take photos of homework questions or assignments for analysis and help
- Chat Support: Share images in chat to ask questions about educational content

Important Privacy Commitment:
- Camera access is optional and only activated when you choose to use camera features
- Photos taken are processed locally on your device
- We do NOT store your photos on our servers without your explicit consent
- We do NOT share your photos with third parties
- You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings

Media/Photo Library Access (android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)
We request access to your photo library to allow you to:
- Select existing photos for homework help
- Upload educational images in chat for questions

Account Information
- Registration Data: Email address, phone number, name, and profile details you provide
- Authentication: Login credentials securely stored and encrypted

Learning and Usage Data
- Progress Data: Quiz scores, completed lessons, and learning achievements
- Educational Content: Questions asked, study materials accessed
- App Usage: Features used and time spent learning

Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version and crash reports for improving stability
- Network status for connectivity features

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- Provide personalized educational services and learning experiences
- Track your progress and celebrate learning achievements
- Improve our app's functionality and user experience
- Send important updates about your account and our services
- Respond to your questions and support requests

INFORMATION SHARING
We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- With your explicit consent
- With service providers who assist in app operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)

DATA SECURITY
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure storage of credentials
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication protocols

DATA RETENTION
We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Qelem is designed for educational use and may be used by children under parental supervision. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
- Parents may review, modify, or delete their child's information by contacting us
- We encourage parental involvement in children's use of the app

YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
- Access and review your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Revoke permissions through your device settings
- Opt-out of promotional communications

CONTACT US
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
- Email: contact@qelem.net
- Website: www.qelem.net

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

By using Qelem, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
