PRIVACY POLICY FOR QELEM
Last updated: Dec 11, 2025

ABOUT QELEM
Qelem is an educational platform designed to provide interactive learning experiences for Ethiopian students of all ages through multiple choice questions (MCQ), flashcards, and progress tracking.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information
- Registration Data: Full name, phone number, username, region, grade level, and profile details you provide during account registration
- Authentication: Login credentials (username and password) securely stored and encrypted
- Profile Information: Any additional profile information you choose to provide

Important: All personal information collected during registration (full name, phone number, username, region, etc.) is used exclusively for app functionality, account management, and service delivery. This information is not shared with or sold to third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

Learning and Usage Data
- Progress Data: Quiz scores, completed lessons, and learning achievements
- Educational Content: Questions asked, study materials accessed
- App Usage: Features used and time spent learning

Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version and crash reports for improving stability
- Network status for connectivity features

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- Provide personalized educational services and learning experiences
- Track your progress and celebrate learning achievements
- Improve our app's functionality and user experience
- Send important updates about your account and our services
- Respond to your questions and support requests

INFORMATION SHARING AND THIRD-PARTY DISCLOSURE

WE DO NOT SELL, RENT, OR SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES FOR MARKETING OR COMMERCIAL PURPOSES.

Your personal information, including but not limited to your full name, phone number, email address, region, grade level, and any other personal data you provide, is collected and used SOLELY FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROVIDING AND IMPROVING OUR EDUCATIONAL SERVICES. We do not monetize your personal data or share it with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

- Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating the App (such as cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processing). These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data for the specific purposes of providing services to us. They are prohibited from using your personal information for their own marketing purposes or sharing it with other parties.

- Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, or that of our users.

- With Your Explicit Consent: When you have explicitly consented to the sharing of your information for a specific purpose.

- Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred, but you will be notified and the new entity will be bound by this Privacy Policy.

We do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising or sell your personal information to data brokers or advertisers.

DATA SECURITY
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure storage of credentials
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication protocols

DATA RETENTION
We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Qelem is designed for educational use and may be used by children under parental supervision. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
- Parents may review, modify, or delete their child's information by contacting us
- We encourage parental involvement in children's use of the app

YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
- Access and review your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Revoke permissions through your device settings
- Opt-out of promotional communications

CONTACT US

- Website: www.qelem.net

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

By using Qelem, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
